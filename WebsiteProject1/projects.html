Jumbled Word Game!

Fun Two-Player Game

A dynamic two-player game where participants take turns attempting to decipher a set of 10 scrambled words. Upon each guess, players receive immediate feedback indicating whether their guess is correct or incorrect, and their scores are adjusted accordingly. After both players have had their turn, they are presented with the option to either continue the challenge or exit the game.

Do you know your Chinese zodiac sign?

Come find it here!

This simple program invites users to input their birth year, instantly calculating and revealing their unique Chinese Zodiac sign. Unveil the ancient wisdom of the Chinese Zodiac and discover which symbolic animal represents you with just a single entry. Experience a seamless fusion of tradition and technology!

Chart of grades

The script uses Python and the matplotlib library to create a pie chart that illustrates the distribution of grades across different assessment components in a course. The chart is visually organized by assigning unique colors to each segment, corresponding to different educational activities like projects, quizzes, midterms, and final exams.
